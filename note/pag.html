About Diploma in information technology

This is the ctevt course which have 3 year of time period. In this course, we can learn skills of hardware maintenance, software development, cyber security, team management, technical speaking etc. This course is academic as well as practical oriented.


Diploma in Information Techmology also we called DIT . DIT has 2 semesters per year which means in 3 years total 6 semesters.
In per semester, minimum of one internal exam which held in own college and one external exam which held in another college.
